WHAT'S NEW?

The AJC has released a new, significantly improved version of the Legislative Navigator Predict-a-bill, the statistical model that estimated the probability that a bill will pass. The new model takes into account where the bill is in the legislative process and how many days are left in the session. In testing on previous sessions the new model has proven much more accurate, and becasue the probability can be calculated daily it reveals how a bill's chances are waning or waxing as the session continues. To learn more, read about the improved model on the AJC Data Team blog
